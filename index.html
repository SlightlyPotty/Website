What We Do

We are a family-run business bringing something creative, fun and Slightly Potty to Ilkeston and the wider area.

Visit our shop to pick out a piece of plain pottery and paint it with your Slightly Potty splodges or perfectlyâ€‘planned design.

Once painted it is left to dry overnight, glazed and then fired in our kiln.

This protects your pot and makes the colours pop! Your creation is then ready for collection.

From plates, bowls and mugs, to money banks, lanterns and animals... We have a wide range of items to spark your creativity!

Who We Are

Wendy is a mother-of-three, Grandmother-of-many, and an ex-nursery-nurse. Wendy loves painting pots, discovering different designs and is more than happy to help with any part of your visit.

Wendy's favourite pot is anything Christmas related!

Whitney is a mother-of-three, nowhere-near-a-Grandmother-yet, and is slightly potty on a calm day. Whitney enjoys dotty designs, but also munching on warm toasties.

Whitney's favourite pot is the mermaid.

Tim is Wendy's youngest son and is potty enough to teach maths when not at the shop. Tim works digitally behind-the-scenes, so that his mum can focus on painting Reindeers and Snowmen all-year-round!

Tim's favourite pot is the dragon.

Emma is Tim's partner and counts newts and bats when not painting pots. Emma can happily spend hours on a small pot, aiming for perfect execution.

Emma's favourite pot is a large plate - a simple blank canvas!
